Beliefs are an essential part of human cognition. People by default have a strong desire for cognitive closure (a made-up mind). Keeping an open mind is difficult, but 

Just comprehending something is often enough to believe it, and therefore to have it affect our behavior. Therefore your [[Default beliefs about any topic are likely unexamined but still affect behavior]].
