Public schools accept a wide variety of folks & are committed to the liberal ideal of not enforcing a particular view on kids. They don't make use of [[Techniques to scale an opinionated educational culture]], except perhaps certain teachers within their specific classrooms.

Absent a clear school-wide culture, public schools may default to reflecting the educational norms of the surrounding culture, which may not be aligned with a school's stated educational aims. Subcultures with differing views on education often form within the school, such as the classic tropes of nerds, jocks, etc.
